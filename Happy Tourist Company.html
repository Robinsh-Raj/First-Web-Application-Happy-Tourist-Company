Welcome to the website of Happy Tours Company ! We embarked on an inspired journey in 1980. We feel a glow of pride to say that over the last 40 years, we have crossed many exciting milestones in Customer Satisfaction.

Here we are happy to present our wide range of Domestic holiday tours that combine meticulous planning, comfortable travel, luxurious accommodation, memorable sightseeing and tantalizing cuisine. Our business is driven by the highest professional and ethical standards, we offer customized service, underlined by personalized attention and care. We make sure that you go back with a truly unforgettable experience and a bagful of happy memories to cherish.

We also have a basket of mobility solutions like Intracity bus service, Intercity buses, Bus and Car rentals, Corporate & School bus services and Hop-on Hop-off city sightseeing services. We thank you for your continued patronage and confidence in us and look forward to taking our relationship with you to a new high.

Warm Regards,

- Tushar Patil (Director)
